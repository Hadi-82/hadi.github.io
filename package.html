What's the trip?

You'll spend three nights in Fira getting the lay of the land, then move on to Oia for the postcard sunsets and slower mornings. We finish with a couple of nights on the quieter east coast at Kamari, where the black-sand beach and family-run tavernas keep the pace blissfully low.

A private boat day around the caldera is included - stops at the Red Beach, the hot springs, and a little island that only the locals seem to know about. Flights, transfers, and breakfasts are all included in the price.
